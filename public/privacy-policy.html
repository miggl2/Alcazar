Alcazar Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Overview

Alcazar is a puzzle game. It does not require an account, login, payment, advertising profile, location, camera, microphone, contacts, or file access.

Data Collection

Alcazar does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data with the developer or third parties.

Local Storage

Alcazar stores puzzle progress, settings, seed values, vibration preference, style preference, and best times locally on your device using browser storage. This data stays on your device and is not sent to the developer.

Children

Alcazar is a general puzzle game and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes

If Alcazar adds accounts, analytics, advertising, payments, cloud sync, crash reporting, or other data collection features, this policy and the Google Play Data safety declaration will be updated before release.
